Privacy Policy

Sannyas Gatherings — Osho Camps & Retreats Directory

This Privacy Policy explains how the Sannyas Gatherings website and app (“the Service”) handle information. The Service is a free, public directory of upcoming Osho meditation camps, retreats and workshops gathered from publicly available sources. Please read this policy to understand our approach to privacy.

In short: the Service is a public directory. It does not require an account, does not ask for your personal details, and does not sell data.

1. Information we collect

The Service is designed to work without collecting personal information from you.

  • No account required. You can browse all listings without signing up or logging in.
  • No personal data requested. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or payment details to use the directory.
  • Optional submissions. If you choose to submit a camp through any form on the Service, the information you enter (such as a camp name, date, location and a contact number for the organiser) is used only to display that listing. Do not submit personal information you do not want shown publicly.

2. Event information shown in the directory

The camp listings shown in the Service are compiled from publicly available sources, including meditation centres’ own websites and public social media posts. Contact numbers shown on listings are those published publicly by the centres or organisers so that interested people can reach them. If you are an organiser and wish to update or remove a listing, please contact us (see Section 8).

3. Device and usage information

The Service is a simple directory and does not include its own analytics or advertising trackers. Like most websites and apps, basic technical information (such as your device type or general region) may be processed by our hosting provider to deliver the site reliably and securely. This is standard server operation and is not used to identify you personally.

4. Installable app (PWA)

The app version of the Service loads the same website inside an app window. It may store a copy of the directory’s files on your device so it can open quickly and work when your connection is poor. This data stays on your device and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing its storage in your device settings.

5. Links to other sites

Listings may link to meditation centres’ websites or their social media posts so you can learn more or register. Those sites are operated by others and have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external sites.

6. Children’s privacy

The Service is a general-audience directory of meditation events and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new “last updated” date below.
